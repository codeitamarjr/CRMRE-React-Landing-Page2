Real Enquiries: The Smarter Way to Verify Tenants

Why Choose Real Enquiries?

Comprehensive Screening: We integrate key public and private data sources, including the RTB (Residential Tenancies Board) for previous disputes, the Bankruptcy Register in the Court of Ireland, and intelligent Google searches for tenancy-related red flags.

Automated Verification: Our AI-driven platform automates data retrieval, reducing manual work and delivering faster, more reliable results.

Customizable Reports: Receive detailed, easy-to-understand reports that summarize a tenant's rental history, financial stability, and potential risks.

GDPR-Compliant Processing: Tenant privacy is a top priority. We ensure compliance with the latest data protection regulations, so you can screen tenants responsibly.

Seamless Integration: Designed to work effortlessly with existing CRM systems, Real Enquiries optimizes your workflow and tenant onboarding process.

A Smarter Alternative to Traditional Screening

Traditional tenant screening methods rely on outdated reference checks and manual calls to previous landlords. Real Enquiries revolutionizes the process with automated AI-driven insights, reducing the risk of fraudulent applications and non-paying tenants. Our real-time data ensures that property managers make informed decisions faster and with greater confidence.

Perfect for Property Professionals

Whether you are managing a single property or an extensive portfolio, Real Enquiries saves you time and money by delivering instant, comprehensive tenant profiles. No more chasing references or waiting days for responses – get the information you need on-demand.
